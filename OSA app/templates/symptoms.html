No Obstructive Sleep Apnea

  • AHI of less than 5 breathing interruptions per hour
  • No significant snoring or breathing interruptions during sleep
  • No significant decrease in oxygen levels in the blood

Mild Obstructive Sleep Apnea

  • AHI of 5 to 15 breathing interruptions per hour
  • Snoring and/or breathing interruptions during sleep
  • No significant decrease in oxygen levels in the blood

Moderate Obstructive Sleep Apnea

  • AHI of 15 to 30 breathing interruptions per hour
  • Snoring and/or breathing interruptions during sleep
  • Mild to moderate decrease in oxygen levels in the blood
  • Possible daytime sleepiness and fatigue

Severe Obstructive Sleep Apnea

  • AHI of more than 30 breathing interruptions per hour
  • Loud snoring and/or frequent breathing interruptions during sleep
  • Significant decrease in oxygen levels in the blood
  • Frequent daytime sleepiness and fatigue
  • Increased risk for serious health complications such as high blood pressure, heart disease, and stroke
